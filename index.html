About Falco

Falco is a light character with polarized movement.

He has the best jumping ability, a fast walking speed, fast falling speed and high air acceleration, but a rather slow dashing speed, below average air speed and very high gravity.

These stats give Falco very slow horizontal movement, but proficient vertical movement.

They also give him a really good vertical survivability for a character of his weight.
